What is Trezo?

Trezo is a cutting-edge, multi-framework admin dashboard template designed to streamline the development of modern web applications. It integrates seamlessly with a variety of popular frameworks, offering developers flexibility and efficiency in their projects.

Is Trezo cloud-based or on-premises?

Trezo is an admin dashboard template that you can integrate into your web applications. Its deployment—whether cloud-based or on-premises—depends on where you host your application. If your application is hosted on cloud servers, Trezo will function in a cloud-based environment; if hosted on local servers, it will operate on-premises. This flexibility allows you to choose the deployment method that best suits your organization's infrastructure and requirements.
